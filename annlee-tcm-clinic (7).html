About the Clinic

Rooted in Tradition,
Guided by Compassion

Dr. Sun Chang brings over a decade of expertise in Traditional Chinese Medicine to Annlee TCM Clinic, offering personalized, evidence-informed care in the heart of Missouri City.

With advanced training in acupuncture, herbal pharmacology, and neurological rehabilitation, Dr. Chang specializes in helping patients recover from strokes, manage chronic and acute pain, and achieve lasting balance across body, mind, and spirit.

Every patient receives a thorough consultation and a treatment plan crafted specifically for their constitution, lifestyle, and health goals — because no two healing journeys are the same.

Our Method

A Thoughtful Four-Step
Path to Healing

01
Comprehensive Assessment
We begin with a thorough intake — reviewing medical history, lifestyle, diet, and your tongue and pulse patterns — to build a complete picture of your health from a TCM perspective.
02
Personalized Diagnosis
Using classical TCM diagnostic principles, Dr. Chang identifies patterns of imbalance — whether Qi stagnation, deficiency, or excess — and translates them into a precise, individualized treatment plan.
03
Integrated Treatment
Combining acupuncture sessions with custom herbal formulas, cupping, and supportive therapies, we address root causes rather than symptoms — stimulating the body's own healing mechanisms.
04
Ongoing Wellness Support
Healing is a journey, not an event. We track your progress, adjust treatments as you improve, and empower you with dietary and lifestyle guidance to sustain your gains long-term.
